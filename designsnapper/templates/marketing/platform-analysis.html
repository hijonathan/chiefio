Read changes, not spreadsheets.

Let chief.io analyze the changes for you and tell you what's important. Rather than digging through spreadsheets of data or a lengthy change history, chief.io gives you a visual comparison.

High-res Screenshots

When chief.io detects a significant change, it takes a high-res screenshot of the page to include in your report. The system then annotates the screenshot, highlighting only the things that are important and cutting out things that are the same or irrelevant.

Smart Recommendations

Changes detected by chief.io are analyzed and classified, so we can tell you exactly how best to respond. You can also create your own classifications and tell chief.io when to use them.

Sort by Anything

Sort your feed of changes by type, date, importance, keyword, or by any combination of the four.
